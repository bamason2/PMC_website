About PMC2022

Powertrain, Modelling and Control biennial international conference on recent advances and developments in new generation electric and hybrid powertrains, covering dynamics, control, testing, characterisation and calibration.

Conference Topics

  • Electric Powertrains
  • Hybrid Powertrains
  • Powertrain System Identification
  • Powertrain Optimisation
  • Controls development and testing
  • Thermal management
  • Hydrogen combustion
  • Hydrogen fuel cell

Previously

Celebrating nearly 10 years of running The Powertrain Modelling and Control (PMC) conference has hosted many globally renown industry speakers and researchers interested in discussing new developments and technologies related to powertrain system engineering.

Venue

How To Get Here

The conference is hosted by Loughborough University East Midlands Campus. Getting to the conference is easy with a range of transportation options available

By Plane

East Midlands Airport is the closest airport, a short 20 minutes taxi ride away from the University

By Car

Access to the University Campus is possible by car, taking the exit at Junction 23 of the M1 motorway

By Bus

Loughborough University is served by frequent busses from the Town Centre

By Train

There are regular services from London and other parts of the country

Facilities & Accommodations

Situated on Campus the Burleigh Court Hotel is the official conference hotel. Other hotels are also available within Loughborough and the surrounding areas.
